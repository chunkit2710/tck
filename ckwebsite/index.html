Introduce

Malaysia is home to a vibrant and diverse indigenous population,
with over 200 different ethnic groups that have lived on the Malay Peninsula and across the island of Borneo for thousands of years.
These indigenous communities, known collectively as the Orang Asli in Peninsular Malaysia and Dayaks in Borneo,
possess rich cultural traditions, languages, and practices that have been passed down through generations.

This website serves as an exploration of the unique lifestyles, histories, and challenges faced by these communities.
From the jungle-dwelling Orang Asli to the culturally rich Iban, Kadazan-Dusun, and Bidayuh of Borneo,
we hope to showcase the diversity and resilience of these remarkable peoples.
